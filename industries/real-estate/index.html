Translation for Dubai Property Transactions

Dubai's real estate market attracts international investors who need their property documents translated for Dubai Land Department (DLD), banks, and legal proceedings. Whether you're purchasing off-plan in Downtown Dubai or selling a villa on Palm Jumeirah, we provide the certified translations that close deals.

Our translators understand Dubai property terminology—from Oqood registrations to Ejari contracts—ensuring your translations are accurate and accepted by all relevant authorities.

Property Documents We Translate

Purchase Documents

  • Title Deeds (Mulkiya)
  • Sale Purchase Agreements
  • Off-plan contracts (Oqood)
  • Handover certificates

Tenancy Documents

  • Tenancy contracts
  • Ejari certificates
  • Addendums
  • Eviction notices

Mortgage & Finance

For Remote Investors

Investing in Dubai property from abroad? We translate Powers of Attorney allowing your representative to sign on your behalf, along with all purchase documents. Everything delivered digitally via WhatsApp—no need to visit Dubai until your keys are ready.
