Complete requirements for all Golden Visa categories. What needs translation, attestation, and notarization.

Quick Overview

The UAE Golden Visa offers 10-year residency for investors, entrepreneurs, specialists, and outstanding students. Each category has specific document requirements.

Translation Requirements Summary

MOJ-Certified Translation: All translations must be certified by UAE Ministry of Justice licensed translators for court/immigration acceptance.

Arabic Translation Required: All non-Arabic/English documents must be translated to Arabic for government submissions.

Original Format Preserved: Translation maintains original document layout, stamps, and signatures visible.
