  • DIFC Courts
  • ADGM Courts
  • Abu Dhabi and other emirate courts
  • Arbitration centers (DIAC, DIFC-LCIA)
‚ùì Do I need attestation before translation? ‚ñº

Depends on the document origin and destination:

  • UAE-issued documents: Usually no attestation needed
  • Foreign documents for UAE use: Typically need home country + UAE embassy attestation first
  • UAE documents for abroad: May need MOFA attestation after translation

We verify requirements before starting and can coordinate attestation if needed.
