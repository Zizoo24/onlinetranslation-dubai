The Remote Handover Pack

Everything you need for a smooth property transfer

Power of Attorney (Bilingual)

Your POA must grant specific powers recognized by UAE law. We translate and ensure it includes: the right to sign on your behalf, receive monies, register with DLD, and handle Ejari/utility transfers.

  • Full buying/selling authority
  • Registration powers at DLD
  • Financial transaction authority
  • Utility account transfers

Title Deed Translation

Your Title Deed (Mulkiya) in Arabic needs to be understood by your bank, solicitor, and potentially foreign tax authorities. We provide a certified translation that captures every detail.

  • Property description and plot details
  • Ownership percentages
  • Encumbrances and mortgages
  • DLD registration numbers

SPA & Oqood Translation

Sale and Purchase Agreements and Oqood (interim registration) documents contain critical terms about payment schedules, handover conditions, and penalties.

  • Payment milestone schedules
  • Handover specifications
  • Developer obligations
  • Penalty and cancellation terms

Also Included:

  • NOC from Developer
  • Service Charge Statements
  • Mortgage Documents
  • Ejari Registration Support
  • DEWA Connection Letters
