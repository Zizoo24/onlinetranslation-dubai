Digital MOFA Attestation

The modern, faster approach for eligible documents

What is e-Attestation?

The UAE has digitized parts of the attestation process. For documents that already have a valid Apostille or embassy stamp, MOFA's digital service can verify and attest documents online, often within 24-48 hours.

Eligible Documents:

  • Documents with valid Apostille from Hague Convention countries
  • Documents already attested by UAE Embassy abroad
  • Educational certificates with digital verification codes

Not Eligible:

  • Documents without prior authentication
  • Documents from non-Hague countries without embassy stamp
  • Documents requiring physical verification

Attestation Pre-Validation

What we check before processing your documents

Document Authenticity

Before we submit any document for attestation, we verify that it appears genuine and complete. MOFA rejects documents with alterations, corrections made with white-out, or missing pages. We catch these issues before they cause delays at the ministry counter.

  • No visible alterations or corrections
  • All pages present and sequential
  • Original signatures (not photocopied)
  • Official letterhead and seals intact

Chain Completeness

Each country has a specific attestation sequence. A missing step in the chain invalidates the entire process. For example, an Indian degree needs SDM notarization before MEA apostille, which must precede UAE Embassy attestation, which must happen before MOFA. We verify your documents have the correct stamps in the correct order.

  • Notary stamp verified
  • Ministry attestation confirmed
  • Embassy/Apostille present
  • Previous stamps not expired

Translation Requirements

Some documents require certified Arabic translation before MOFA attestation; others can be attested first then translated. The order matters for cost and efficiency. We advise whether to translate before or after attestation based on your specific document type and end use.

  • Translation timing optimized
  • MOJ certification requirements checked
  • Bilingual document handling
  • Cost-effective sequencing advised

Validity Periods

Attestation stamps have validity periods. An apostille older than 3 months may be rejected by some UAE authorities. Police clearance certificates typically expire 6-12 months from issuance depending on country. We verify your documents are within acceptance windows before processing.

  • Apostille date verified
  • Embassy stamp validity checked
  • Document issuance date confirmed
  • Expiry warnings provided
