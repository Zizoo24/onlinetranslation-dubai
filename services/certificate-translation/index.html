What You Need to Know

Do I need the original certificate?

For translation only, a clear scan or photo is sufficient. If you need attestation afterwards, the original will be required for stamp placement.

Is the translation accepted by all UAE authorities?

Yes. Our translations are certified under MOJ license and accepted by Dubai Courts, ICP, MOFA, banks, schools, and all government ministries across the UAE.

My certificate is in a language other than English. Can you translate it?

Absolutely. We translate certificates from Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, Tagalog, French, German, Russian, Chinese, and 50+ other languages to English and Arabic.

What if my certificate is old or damaged?

We can work with most documents regardless of age. If portions are illegible, we'll note that in the translation. For severely damaged documents, we may recommend obtaining a replacement from the issuing authority first.
