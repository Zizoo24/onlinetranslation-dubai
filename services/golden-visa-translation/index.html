Golden Visa Compliance Checklist

Before we accept your documents, we verify these critical requirements

Attestation Chain Verification

We check that your documents have the correct attestation sequence before translation. A degree from India requires: HRD (Notary) → MEA → UAE Embassy in Delhi → MOFA Dubai. A UK degree requires: Solicitor Certification → FCO Apostille → MOFA Dubai. Missing any step means the translated document will be rejected by ICP, regardless of translation quality.

  • Home country notarization verified
  • Foreign ministry attestation confirmed
  • UAE Embassy stamp checked
  • MOFA attestation status confirmed

Name Consistency Check

ICP systems flag applications where names differ between documents. We cross-reference all names against your passport to ensure exact matches. If your degree says "Mohammed" but your passport says "Mohamed," we note this discrepancy and advise on the correction affidavit process before you waste time on a rejected application.

  • Passport name as primary reference
  • Degree certificate name matching
  • Marriage certificate name alignment
  • Spelling variations documented

Document Quality Assessment

Faded stamps, partial scans, or low-resolution images cause ICP upload failures. We assess every document for scan quality, stamp visibility, and page completeness. If your bank statement is missing page 3 of 6, we tell you before wasting translation fees on an incomplete submission.

  • Minimum 300 DPI scan quality
  • All pages present and numbered
  • Official stamps clearly visible
  • No cropped signatures or seals

Validity Period Verification

Bank statements must cover the most recent 6 months. Salary certificates should be dated within 30 days of submission. Police clearance certificates have country-specific validity windows (FBI certificates are valid for 6 months; UK ACRO certificates for 12 months). We verify your documents are within the acceptable timeframes for ICP processing.

  • Bank statement date range confirmed
  • Salary certificate issuance date checked
  • PCC validity window verified
  • Document expiry alerts provided

Golden Visa Category Requirements

Each visa category has specific documentation requirements

Specialized Talent (Doctors, Engineers, Scientists)

The specialized talent category is the most common route for high-earning professionals. The Ministry of Human Resources (MOHRE) defines "specialized" as professions requiring advanced education and earning above AED 30,000 monthly.

Required Documents:
  • University Degree: Must be from an accredited institution with full attestation chain. See degree translation requirements
  • Professional License: DHA license for doctors, SCE stamp for engineers, or equivalent regulatory body certification
  • Employment Contract: Current contract showing job title, salary, and "unlimited" or "permanent" status
  • Salary Certificate: Dated within 30 days, specifying monthly salary in AED
  • 6-Month Bank Statements: Showing consistent salary deposits. Bank statement translation details

Processing typically takes 2-3 business days for translation, plus 1-2 weeks for MOE equivalency if your degree requires it.

Real Estate Investor (AED 2 Million Property)

Property investors with off-plan or completed properties valued at AED 2 million or more qualify for the 10-year Golden Visa. Joint ownership is permitted for married couples.

Required Documents:
  • Title Deed (Mulkiya): From Dubai Land Department showing property value
  • Property Valuation: If off-plan, developer letter confirming investment value
  • Purchase Agreement: SPA showing payment schedule and total value
  • Marriage Certificate: If joint ownership with spouse. Marriage certificate translation
  • Passport Copies: All pages, current validity

Note: Properties with mortgage are acceptable as long as equity meets the AED 2 million threshold.

Entrepreneur (Business Owner)

Business owners with approved projects or existing businesses meeting revenue thresholds can qualify. This includes startup founders with MOEC approval and established business owners with audited financials.

Required Documents:
  • Trade License: Active license showing business activity
  • Memorandum of Association: Showing ownership percentage. MOA translation details
  • Audited Financial Statements: Past 2-3 years showing revenue
  • Bank Statements: Business account showing transaction volume
  • MOEC Approval Letter: For startup category applicants
Family Sponsorship (Dependents)

Golden Visa holders can sponsor spouse, children (no age limit), and parents. Each dependent requires their own document set translated and submitted alongside the main applicant.

Required Documents per Dependent:
  • Marriage Certificate: For spouse sponsorship, attested and translated. View requirements
  • Birth Certificate: For children, showing parent names. Birth certificate translation
  • Passport Copies: All family member passports
  • Current Photos: White background, passport specifications
  • School Records: For children currently enrolled in UAE schools
